# Vision

## Mission
AI Video Studio will become the most intuitive AI-powered video editing platform for creators, agencies, and businesses that need professional results without sacrificing speed or simplicity.

## Long-Term Vision
We envision a global platform where users can describe the edit they want in natural language and receive polished video content in minutes. The product will blend the ease of modern creator tools with the depth of professional editing suites, while making advanced AI assistance available through both local and cloud workflows.

## Target Users
- Individual creators producing content for social media and short-form video
- Marketing teams creating campaigns and promotional content
- Agencies producing client deliverables at scale
- Education and internal communications teams
- Enterprise customers needing managed workflows and API access

## Product Goals
- Reduce editing time from hours to minutes
- Make advanced video editing accessible to non-experts
- Provide professional-grade output quality
- Support hybrid local/cloud processing for flexibility and cost control
- Enable future extensibility through APIs and multi-provider AI integrations

## Guiding Principles
- AI-first workflow design
- Mobile-first experience
- Modular and enterprise-ready architecture
- Human-in-the-loop control for critical edits
- Transparent, explainable AI assistance
